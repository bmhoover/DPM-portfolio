About Me

Hi, I'm Britt Hoover, a May 2025 graduate from the University of Iowa with a B.B.A in Business Analytics and Information Systems and a minor in Psychology. I have a strong interest in understanding human behavior and how data can be used to uncover insights about people and decision-making.

My Background

I am from Chicago, IL, and attended Loyola Academy for high school. At the University of Iowa, I was a member of Delta Delta Delta and was involved in both the marketing committee and the DEI committee. Studying Business Analytics and Psychology has helped me develop both technical and analytical skills, which I am excited to apply in my career.
